Our Story

Bringing the precision of the professional kitchen into the warmth of the home.

Mike & Raf

We've been friends and colleagues since 2019, when we first met working at Cardinal Spirits. Over the years, we have been integral parts of the Bloomington food scene, refining our crafts in some of the city's most respected kitchens.

As founding members of The Elm's culinary team, we worked alongside Chef Dan Thomas to establish a haven for foodies. We learned that world-class dining isn't just about the food on the plate, it's about the memory of the evening.

Your Kitchen, Our Expertise.

"We wanted to take everything we loved about the restaurant industry; the technique, the hospitality, the seasonal inspiration, and remove the walls of the restaurant."
