Frequently Asked Questions

How does the process work?

Once you submit an inquiry, we'll reach out to discuss dates and menu preferences. On the day of the event, we arrive 1.5 to 2 hours before the first course to prep in your kitchen. We serve each course, explain the dishes, and handle the complete cleanup of the kitchen and dining area before we depart.

What do I need to provide?

You provide the kitchen, the oven/stove, and the guests! We bring all the specialized equipment, ingredients, and cleaning supplies. We generally use your plates and silverware to maintain the "at-home" feel, but we can discuss rentals if you need specific service ware.

Do you provide alcohol or wine pairings?

Due to local licensing regulations, we cannot sell or provide alcohol. However, we are happy to suggest wine pairings or cocktail recipes that complement your specific menu so you can have them ready for your guests.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Because every menu is custom-built, we can accommodate allergies, gluten-free needs, or vegetarian preferences. Please let us know about any restrictions during the initial inquiry so we can design the menu accordingly.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Monroe County. If you are located just outside the county, feel free to reach out—travel fees may apply depending on the distance.
