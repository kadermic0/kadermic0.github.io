Frequently Asked Questions

How does the process work?

Once you submit an inquiry, we'll reach out to discuss dates and menu preferences. On the day of the event, we arrive 1.5 to 2 hours before the first course to prep. We serve each course, explain the dishes, and handle the complete cleanup.

What do I need to provide?

You provide the kitchen and the guests! We bring specialized equipment, ingredients, and cleaning supplies. We generally use your plates and silverware to maintain the "at-home" feel.

Do you provide alcohol?

We cannot sell or provide alcohol. However, we are happy to suggest wine pairings or cocktail recipes that complement your specific menu.
